About Me

Data Scientist

Washington DC, USA

I have five years of experience as a data scientist and statistician, with the last three years being focused on Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. I have a Bachelor's and an MSc in Economics with a strong quantitative background.

I am interested in applications of Machine Learning and Deep Learning for social good and on topics related to multilinguality, interpretability, and detecting and avoiding bias in algorithms.

Currenlty, I work as a data scientist at the Inter-American Development Bank (IADB). The IADB is a multilateral organization that that finances development projects in Latin America and the Caribbean. I work in the Knowledge, Innovation and Communications Division applying different NLP techniques for knowledge management.
